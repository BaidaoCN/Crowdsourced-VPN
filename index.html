📘 What is OpenVPN?

🔒 OpenVPN is a secure and flexible VPN protocol that creates encrypted tunnels between your device and the VPN server. To use this service, you need an OpenVPN client (e.g., OpenVPN Connect or Tunnelblick on macOS, or the official OpenVPN client for Windows/Linux).

📋 How to connect:
1️⃣ Request credentials for an online router below.
2️⃣ Download the OpenVPN configuration file (provided after credential request).
3️⃣ Import the .ovpn file into your OpenVPN client and enter the username/password.
4️⃣ Connect and enjoy private, decentralized VPN access.

✨ This platform is free and crowdsourced – contributed by volunteers.
